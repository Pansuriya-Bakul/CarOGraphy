User Guide

Welcome to Carography, your go-to car service application! This user guide will walk you through the features and functionalities of our app.

Features

  • Schedule service appointments for your vehicle.
  • View and manage your vehicle's service history.
  • Receive reminders for upcoming maintenance tasks.
  • Find nearby service centers and garages.
  • Get real-time updates on the status of your vehicle's service.

Getting Started

To get started with Carography, follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit our website at www.carography.com.
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button located in the top right corner of the page.
  3. Fill out the registration form with your details, including your name, email address, and desired password.
  4. Click on the "Create Account" button to complete the registration process.
  5. Once registered, log in to your account using your email address and password.
  6. You will be redirected to your dashboard, where you can start exploring the features of Carography.

If you encounter any issues during the registration or login process, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.

Booking a Service

To book a service with Carography, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Carography account.
  2. From the dashboard, navigate to the "Book Service" section.
  3. Select the type of service you require (e.g., oil change, tire rotation).
  4. Choose your preferred date and time for the service appointment.
  5. Provide any additional details or specific requirements for the service.
  6. Confirm your booking details and proceed to payment (if applicable).
  7. Once booked, you will receive a confirmation email with the appointment details.

If you encounter any issues during the registration or login process, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.

Booking a Service

To book a service with Carography, follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Carography account.
  2. Go to the "Service History" section in your dashboard.
  3. You will see a list of all previous service appointments and maintenance tasks.
  4. To view details of a specific service entry, click on it to expand.

If you encounter any issues during the registration or login process, please don't hesitate to contact our support team for assistance.

FAQs and Troubleshooting

Below are some frequently asked questions and troubleshooting tips:

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. Q: How do I reset my password?
    A: You can reset your password by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions sent to your email.

  2. Q: Can I schedule multiple service appointments for different vehicles?
    A: Yes, you can schedule service appointments for multiple vehicles from your account dashboard.

  3. Q: How do I update my profile information?
    A: You can update your profile information, including name, email, and contact details, from the "Account Settings" section in your dashboard.

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Issue: Unable to log in to my account
    - Ensure you are entering the correct email address and password.
    - Check if your internet connection is stable.
    - Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try logging in again.
  • Issue: Not receiving email notifications
    - Check your spam or junk email folder.
    - Add our email address to your contacts or whitelist to ensure delivery.
    - Contact our support team if the issue persists.
  • Issue: Error messages or unexpected behavior
    - Refresh the page and try again.
    - Clear your browser cache and cookies.
    - Try accessing the application from a different browser or device.
    - If the issue persists, contact our support team for assistance.
